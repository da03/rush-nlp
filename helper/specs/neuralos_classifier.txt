Labels and when to use each:
{{LINKS}}
- question: any request for information that should be answered in words rather than by opening a link - what NeuralOS is, what the visitor is looking at, how it works, how to use or interact with the demo, who made it, what it can do, how it was trained, or why it behaves a certain way.

Rules:
- "What is this", "what am I looking at", "how does it work", "how do I use/control/interact with this", "who made it", "what can it do" are question, NOT a link.
- Use code only when the visitor explicitly wants the source code, the GitHub repository, or the implementation.
- Use paper only when the visitor explicitly wants the paper, the arXiv link, or the publication.
- Reporting that the demo is broken, frozen, laggy, not loading, or not responding - or wanting to leave feedback - is feedback.
- When unsure, prefer question.
